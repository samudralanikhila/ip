    5.difference b/w block and inline elements.
      block: The block level element allways begin in new line and it extends from left to right .
      It take the full width available on the screen. 
      Browsers automatically adds some space(margin) and before and after the element.
      Example: h1 to h6, div , p, ol, ul , table, etc
    
      Inline: Does not begin on a new line. It only takes the space is necessary for it.
      Example: span, img, input, a, lable, etc,
